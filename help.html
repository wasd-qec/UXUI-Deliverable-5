Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place an order? +
Browse our menu, tap the + button to add items to your cart, then proceed to checkout. You can choose pickup or delivery to your room.
What are the delivery hours? +
We deliver from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Orders placed after 9:00 PM will be scheduled for the next morning.
How do I use my points? +
At checkout, check the "Use points" option to apply your accumulated points as a discount. 100 points = $1 off.
Can I cancel my order? +
Orders can be cancelled within 2 minutes of placing them. After that, please contact our support team for assistance.
How do I apply a voucher? +
Go to Settings > Vouchers to view and apply available vouchers. You can also enter a voucher code manually.
